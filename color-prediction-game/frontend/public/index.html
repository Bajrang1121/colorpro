How to Play

1. Place Your Bet

Choose a color (Red, Green, Blue, or Violet) and enter the amount you want to bet. Minimum bet is ₹100 and maximum is ₹10,000.

2. Wait for Result

Each round lasts 60 seconds. Once the timer reaches zero, the winning color is randomly selected.

3. Collect Winnings

If your predicted color matches the winning color, you win! Red pays 2.5x, Green pays 3x, Blue pays 4x, and Violet pays 5x your bet amount.

Tips for Success

  • Start with smaller bets to understand the game
  • Keep track of recent results to identify patterns
  • Set a budget and stick to it
  • Take advantage of our welcome bonus for new players
